## Risk Assessment

### Acquisition Integration Risk ⚠️ **CRITICAL**
- Acquired ND Western 40% additional stake (now owns ~55%)
- Integration complexity: different systems, cultures, processes
- Debt load increased 20x (₦96B → ₦2.00T)
- Execution track record unproven at this scale

### Commodity Price Risk ⚠️ **HIGH**
- Revenue tied to oil/gas prices
- 2025 revenue breakdown: Crude Oil (63%), Gas (7%), Refined Products (30%)
- Brent price sensitivity: Every $10/bbl swing ≈ 15-20% profit impact

### Leverage Risk ⚠️ **HIGH**
- Debt-to-Equity jumped from 7% (2024) to 58% (2025)
- Interest coverage: ~17x (manageable but deteriorating)
- Refinancing risk on ₦2T debt load

### Nigerian Political Risk ⚠️ **MODERATE**
- Asset concentration in Niger Delta
- PIA transition uncertainty
- FX volatility exposure
