## Business Overview

### Company Structure
Aradel Holdings PLC is a Nigerian integrated oil & gas company with four operating subsidiaries:

1. **Aradel Energy** - E&P operations (OML 54, OML 53, OML 34, OPL 227)
2. **Aradel Gas** - Gas processing (100mmscf/d facility supplying NLNG)
3. **Aradel Refineries** - 11kbbl/d refinery at Ogbele field
4. **Aradel Investments** - Non-oil investments

### Asset Portfolio

| Asset | Type | Status |
|-------|------|--------|
| OML 54 (Ogbele) | Onshore marginal field | Producing |
| OML 53 (Omerelu) | Onshore marginal field | Producing |
| OML 34 | Onshore | Producing |
| OPL 227 | Shallow water | Exploration |

### Competitive Advantages (Moat Analysis)

**STRONG:**
- **Strategic Gas Infrastructure**: 100mmscf/d gas processing facility with NLNG offtake
- **Vertical Integration**: Upstream + Midstream + Downstream (refining)
- **Nigerian Content Champion**: Local content advantage in regulatory environment
- **Reserve Base**: Diversified across multiple licenses

**MODERATE:**
- **Refining Capacity**: 11kbbl/d provides product upgrading capability
- **Gas Monetization**: NLNG supply contract provides stable revenue

**WEAKNESS:**
- **Commodity Exposure**: Pure oil/gas price play
- **Nigerian Risk**: Asset location concentration
- **Capital Intensity**: Heavy reinvestment requirements

## Risk Assessment

### Commodity Price Risk ⚠️ **CRITICAL**
- Revenue directly tied to oil/gas prices
- 2022 collapse (N66B→N15B profit) demonstrates volatility
- Oil at $100+ drove 2023 results—what happens at $60?

### Nigerian Political Risk ⚠️ **HIGH**
- Asset concentration in Niger Delta
- Regulatory uncertainty (PSC terms, taxes)
- FX volatility (Naira devaluation risk)

### Reserve Depletion Risk ⚠️ **MODERATE**
- Oil & gas are depleting assets
- Must continuously replace reserves
- OPL 227 exploration success uncertain

### Financial Engineering Risk ⚠️ **MODERATE**
- 2022 tax credit (N18B) artificially boosted results
- PSC accounting can obscure true economics
- Need detailed balance sheet to assess debt

### Capital Intensity ⚠️ **HIGH**
- E&P requires constant reinvestment
- Refinery margins thin at 11kbbl/d scale
- Gas infrastructure has limited alternative uses
